About

LangUse contains a set of exercises to practice the Dutch language specifically prepared for Inburgering exam. Only using LangUse is not enough to learn the Dutch grammar rules but it is more than enough to practice them to pass the exam

  • Learn More

Features

  • Telegram bot

    You can practice the exercises whenever and wherever you want via the messenger you have.

  • Website

    You can configure everything via a dedicated website with your telegram login.

  • Custom dictionary

    You can gradually fill in your own dictionary of words you know from the full dictionary of words from the exam.

How it works

  • 5 120 Words
  • 10 Free exercises
  • 41 Paid exercises
  • 9 euro per month
  • 5 happy users

Let's take for example one exercise: practicing Dutch pronouns. You will start from selecting on the website the Dutch pronouns you've already known. Then you can choose how many times per day you want to practice them. Next day you will get the pronoun in English and you will need to translate it. Forget the translation? No problem, you can always ask bot to translate the word you forgot. Okay, after a week you practiced all Dutch pronouns and know them by heart now. Add another exercise to practice Dutch pronouns and verbs. Choose the verbs you know from the dictionary. Now every exercise you will get a pair of words in English: pronoun + verb. Go ahead: add new words to your dictionary, add new exercises and practice the exam in a simple and fun manner!
